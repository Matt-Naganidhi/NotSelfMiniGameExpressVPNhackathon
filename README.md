
## Project Overview

EQ-Gate is a "Secure by Design" enhancement that adds an Emotional Readiness layer to traditional age verification.

Instead of just checking a birthdate, it uses a gamified assessment to verify that a young user actually understands how to handle cyberbullying, respect digital identities, and spot online predators before they gain access. 

By measuring maturity alongside identity, EQ-Gate ensures that safety is built into the platform's architecture, protecting users through education without requiring invasive content monitoring or extra data collection.

(Developed for the FOSSASIA Hackathon 2026, in Bangkok Thailand (Theme - Secure by Design: Privacy-First Digital Safety for Young Users))

## How it works

Think of EQ-Gate as a "digital driver’s license" test that sits right after age verification. While a birthdate tells you how old a user is, EQ-Gate proves they are actually ready to be there. 

By putting users through a quick, gamified series of social scenarios—like spotting a bully or a "secret-sharing" stranger—it verifies their digital maturity before they ever hit the "Join" button. This creates a secure-by-design environment where safety isn't just a rule in the Terms of Service, but a verified skill required for entry, all without needing to monitor a single private message.
